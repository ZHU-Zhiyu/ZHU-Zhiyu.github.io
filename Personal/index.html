Zhiyu Zhu is a PostDoc. reseacher of computer science at the Visual Information Processing Group Cityu HK, led by Prof. Junhui HOU. His research interests primarily lie in the field of computer vision, which covers both high-level and low-level topics, i.e., object detection and tracking, as well as image super-resolution and reconstruction. He has also published a number of papers in prestigious conferences and journals, including ICCV, NeurIPS, CVPR, ACM MM, TIP, TCI.
